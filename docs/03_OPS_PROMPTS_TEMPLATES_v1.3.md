
# Ops Prompts & Time-Saver Rules v1.3

---

## A. Motion Prompt Templates (Text Only)

### Premium — Logo Loop
Use exact reference. No redesign.

- 3–6s seamless loop
- subtle glow / smoke
- no camera movement
- no text warping

---

### Elite — Seasonal Loop
Animate decorations only.
Logo remains static, sharp, centered.

---

### Elite — Template Loop
Background motion only.
Text area must remain readable.

---

## B. Watermark Policy

If demo watermark = YES:
- watermark all previews
- watermark all edits
- watermark all finals
- reapply after every regeneration

If “remove watermark for finals” = YES:
- drafts remain watermarked
- finals are clean

---

## C. Time-Saver Rules

- One deliverable per message.
- If no edits requested, export and move on.
- Motion prompts generated **after** static assets are locked.
- Do not explain decisions during production.
